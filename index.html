Fuel hope for single-parent households & veterans December 1–12

Every winter we rally urgent relief for caregivers raising children alone and service members returning to civilian life. Your donation keeps heat flowing, meals stocked, counseling funded, and dignity restored—right when it’s needed most.

This year’s focus

Emergency grants averaging $500 per household, trauma-informed counseling sessions, and mobile pantry deliveries that stretch every dollar where it counts.

Where your gift lands first

We listen to partner agencies in Atlanta, Houston, Fayetteville, and San Diego to dispatch support within 24 hours. Two communities lead our mission this season.

Single-parent households

71% of the families we serve are led by moms. Utilities, child care, and transportation threaten their stability long before holiday wish lists. Your generosity delivers:

  • Emergency rent + utility catch-up
  • Weekend grocery bundles & diapers
  • Gift cards for winter clothing

Military veterans

We collaborate with VA peer mentors to reach veterans navigating PTSD, unemployment, or housing gaps. During 12 Days of Giving we fund:

  • Telehealth therapy stipends
  • Hotel vouchers and warm gear
  • Small business micro-grants

Rapid response workflow

Every donation is processed securely through Stripe, logged in our transparency dashboard, and issued to partners within 48 hours. You’ll receive a receipt plus daily progress highlights.

12 days. 12 acts of radical care.

Follow along as we unlock a new initiative each day. Share the schedule, sponsor a day, or stack matching funds with your company.

Day 01

Heat & Light Fund

Keep power on for 40 single-parent households facing shutoff notices.

Day 02

Grocery Dash

Deploy mobile pantry trucks stocked with culturally familiar staples.

Day 03

Honor Flights Home

Cover travel for veterans attending urgent medical appointments.

Day 04

Childcare Boost

Provide pop-up childcare stipends so parents can work extra shifts.

Day 05

Warm Gear Kits

Deliver coats, boots, and thermal layers to unhoused veterans.

Day 06

Therapy Sessions

Fund 120 trauma-informed telehealth appointments.

Day 07

Teen Tech Lab

Refurbish laptops for teens in single-parent homes.

Day 08

Emergency Transport

Gas cards and rideshare credits to get families to work and school.

Day 09

Veteran Ventures

Micro-grants for veteran-owned startups covering tooling and permits.

Day 10

Holiday Tables

Community dinners with chef partners & live music.

Day 11

Rest & Respite

Weekend hotel stays for caregivers in burnout.

Day 12

Legacy Scholarships

College savings deposits honoring veterans and their children.
